Catering: Contact us

Check out our gallery



Send us an Email at contact@charlees-comfort-kitchen.com

Please include Name, Phone Number and answer all questions below

1- How many people do you intend on feeding?

2- When and where will this event take place? Date, time, place.

3- What kind of budget do you have? This can be a total for the entire event or even what you’re looking to spend per person. (please keep in mind there will be sales tax and depending on your individual needs, possible set up fees, rental fees, etc…)

4- Are you looking for someone to bring and set up or will you just be picking up food that is ready to go and/or easy to reheat and serve later?

5- If you would like us to come and set up, will there be a kitchen accessible for heating and cooking food or will we need to bring our own equipment?

6- Do you have all necessary serving items such as platters, chafers/crock pots/heat lamps for keeping food warm, serving utensils, plates & utensils for guests, etc… or will those items need to be provided for you?

7- What type of menu are you looking for? Here is just a few examples:
Light grazing buffet: mostly cold/room temp finger foods & hors d’oeuvres
Light full meal buffet: most cold/room temp items but much more filling
“One and done”: one entrée/main, one side, one dessert
Heavy full meal buffet: mixture of hot and cold items with more substance
Plated dinner: high end fancy coursed meal
Breakfast meeting: coffee, juices, pastries, breakfast burritos and/or sandwiches
Wedding cake? If so, please give a few details of what you’re looking for
